Our Facilities

State-of-the-art manufacturing infrastructure and equipment

Stamping Infrastructure & Equipment

Our advanced manufacturing facilities are equipped with cutting-edge technology and machinery to deliver superior quality products.

Press Capacity

Dyna-K Automotive Stampings Private Limited. maintains Mechanical/Hydraulic Presses of a capacity of 250 to 6500 KN with 50 Presses at Facility 1(J6) and 10 Presses at Facility 2 (J450). The fleet of progressive presses offer the best in quality and speed.

We are constantly upgrading our equipment to meet the current demands of the stamping industry as well as continually training our employees and machine operators in the latest manufacturing technologies.

Keeping up with our ever-growing customer base needs, we are expanding and have acquired an additional + 28000 sq. ft. as well.

Capabilities

  • Material Shearing & Pre-Processing Facility
  • Tool Design & Manufacturing
  • Projection Welding & MIG Welding
  • Raw Material Shearing / Pre-Processing
  • Welding & Tapping centre
  • Vibro-Deburring
  • Washing

Tooling & Design Capabilities

Our in-house tool design and manufacturing capabilities ensure superior quality and precision in every component.

Advanced Tooling

The High Quality Single Stage & Progressive Tools make Dyna-K Automotive Stampings Private Limited one of the best precision stamping companies in India.

The Product development process is supported by an in-house Tool Design team & Tool Manufacturing facility, monitored by a cross-functional APQP (Advance Product Quality Planning) team and also supported by FMEA/MSA/SPC activities.

After reviewing customer needs, the team focuses on process excellence, innovation and validation transposing customer data of many forms into articulate designs.

Key Features

  • Designed & Manufactured In-House
  • Tool Design through Autocad & 3D Modelling Software (Solid Edge)
  • High Quality Steels & Components
  • Vacuum Heat Treated / TiN & CrN & WC Coated

Quality Systems

Our commitment to quality is validated by comprehensive testing and monitoring systems.
