Company Profile

Dyna-K Automotive Stamping Pvt. Ltd Private Limited was founded in December 1985, to cater to the requirements of precision stampings & pressed components for the automotive industry. Over the years, Dyna-K Automotive Stamping Pvt. Ltd Private Limited capitalized on its tool design capability to cater to added automotive requirements and safety requirements of stampings for Brake components, Welded Assemblies, Engine Parts, Turbo Charger parts etc.

Since commencing operation, Dyna- K has achieved many milestones and emerged as one of the preferred suppliers. Our partnership as a supplier commences from the early tool design stage to production and supply. With robust manufacturing driven by TQM , Lean practices and required investment in technology we have built strategic partnerships with Suppliers.

Dyna-K Automotive Stamping Pvt. Ltd Private Limited is equipped with a stamping & assembly facility which is spread in 2 plants with an approx. 52,000 sq. ft. area of workshops in Pune MIDC Industrial Area. Coupled with in-house tool design & manufacturing, Dyna-K Automotive Stamping Pvt. Ltd Private Limited is a IATF 16949 Certified Quality Organisation and geared to meet and exceed its customer expectations.
