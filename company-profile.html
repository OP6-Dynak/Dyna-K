About

Dyna-K Automotive Stamping Pvt. Ltd was founded in December 1985, to cater to the requirements of precision stampings & pressed components for the automotive industry. Over the years, Dyna-K Automotive Stamping Pvt. Ltd capitalized on its tool design capability to cater to added automotive requirements and safety requirements of stampings for Brake components, Chassis Parts, Engine Parts, Emission Parts, Filtration Parts, Passive Safety Components, Turbo Charger Parts, Welded Assemblies Parts etc.

Since commencing operation, Dyna-K has achieved many milestones and emerged as one of the preferred suppliers. Our partnership as a supplier commences from the early tool design stage to production and supply. With robust manufacturing driven by TQM, Lean practices and required investment in technology we have built strategic partnerships with Suppliers.

Dyna-K Automotive Stamping Pvt. Ltd is equipped with a stamping & assembly facility which is spread in 2 plants with an approx. 55,000 sq. ft. area of workshops in Pune MIDC Industrial Area. Coupled with in-house tool design & manufacturing, Dyna-K Automotive Stamping Pvt. Ltd is a IATF 16949 Certified Quality Organisation and geared to meet and exceed its customer expectations.

Our Journey Through The Years

Explore our growth and achievements since our founding in 1985

  • 1985

    Dyna-K commissions Plant 1 for indigenizing back plates with support from Suzuki/Bharat Forge

  • 1996

    Dyna-K commissions Plant 2 for indigenizing Booster Shells for Chakan Plant

  • 1998

    Approved as QS 9000 Company Started Supplying Parts to Huddersfield CTT-US,UK,Brazil,China,India Plants. Set up VMI Warehouse in Charleston, US & Huddersfield, UK

  • 2003

    Approved as TS 16949 by DQS

  • 2005

    KBX acquisition by Bosch

  • 2009
